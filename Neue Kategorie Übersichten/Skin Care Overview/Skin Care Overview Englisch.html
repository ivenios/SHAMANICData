Alle Skin Care Produkte von SHAMANIC The Oil

 

We believe that nature’s energy can be transferred to your charisma and skin health. After more than 10 years of research and subsequent experiments with nearly 1000 oils, we know that this can only succeed by using pure and intelligently combined natural oils. The ideal skin and hair care is based on pure oil formulations which optimize the water balance of the skin and hair. We only use raw materials gathered through wild harvesting from the Amazon region. Harvesting is done according to old shamanic criteria and we only harvest as much as nature can spare. We process these valuable ingredients cold-pressed and our active oils are unrefined. In our Starnberg manufactory we compose them into highly effective, BDIH-certified products according to the latest scientific findings. All SHAMANIC products are anhydrous and vegan throughout.

Harvested from the wild in Brazil. Unrefined active oils. Cold-pressed. Vegan. Waterless. BDIH certified. Made in Germany.
